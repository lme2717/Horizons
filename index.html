AUTODRAWER

Making a chest of drawers for back pain sufferers

The Problem

Back pain is an issue that means the daily task of reaching for low shelves/drawers is problematic. It affects many people, with one study showing that musculoskeletal pain in older adults (65+) ranges from 65-85%, with 36-70% of them suffering from back pain. (A.Y.L. Wong et al. 2017). Our design aims to reduce the need to bend down by raising low level storage units to a comfortable height.

Our Solution

A Storage unit that brings the drawer to the user

We're currently protyping a mechanism that will select specific lower drawers and lift them up to the same level as the top drawers. This will happen at the push of a button, and all moving parts will be enclosed so it is safe for all. Details of the mechanism can be found by clicking on 'our solution' above.

Our Team

We are a group of four Mechanical Engineering Students at Imperial College London prototyping this product in Imperial's Advanced Hackspace.
